About The Group

Overview & Mission

Founded in July 2022 and proudly 100% Liberian-owned, SSG is a dynamic business conglomerate headquartered in Chubor, Montserrado County. Our mission is to empower communities and drive national development through reliable, quality, and affordable services across a wide range of sectors.

We are committed to delivering trustworthy, innovative, and people-centered services that improve lives and build national capacity.
